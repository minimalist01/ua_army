IF|Help

A non-profit organization whose mission is to create a culture of volunteerism and mutual assistance in Ukraine.

Why do we volunteer?
We want to live in a successful country. And a country is defined by its people. When everyone cares about their surroundings and solves problems that concern them, the country develops. Volunteers are ordinary people who give their time, talent or resources to socially useful projects. Everyone can do good deeds: children, adults, people with special needs, companies, institutions, and even entire countries.
Even one good deed can change a society, a country, and the world for the better. Today, volunteer organizations exist in 80 countries. About 110 million people annually participate in voluntary programs where they help in the areas of education, healthcare, environment, social protection and many others.

What are we doing?

1.
We study the needs of the civilian population
2.
We create a project to find funding for the identified needs
3.
We get what we need from donors or buy it.
4.
We receive an official request for help from the population
5.
We communicate with the recipient to clarify details and prepare documents
6.
We organize the logistics of the aid
